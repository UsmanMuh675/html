Fried Rice

Delicious homemade fried rice that is easy to cook.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 20 mins

Servings: 2 people

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Vegetable oil
  • Eggs
  • Carrots
  • Green peas
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Boil the rice
  2. Heat oil in a pan
  3. Add vegetables and fry
  4. Add eggs and mix
  5. Add rice and stir well

Image

Note

Serve hot for best taste.
